"I give him the apple" translated by a native speaker to find out the building blocks of grammar in a foreign language.This project is to get experienced teachers to translate these sentences (with audio!) so you can break them down. The resource is inspired by Tim Ferriss' language learning method, which he outlines in his great book The 4 Hour Chef

Translations and audio by Angel - a native French teacher from Dijon, France. He has been teaching for over 11 years. He runs frenchlover.org and offers lessons on iTalki.
